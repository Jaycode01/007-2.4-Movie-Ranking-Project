The Best Movies According to Joe

My top 5 movies of all-time.

WarGames

This classic film is one of the best to address cybersecurity, where a computer scientist unintentionally connects his computer to the U.S Department of Defense. This movie is made in 1983 .

The Net

Starring Sandra Bullock, this movie follows a computer exert who discover a program that allows access to secret databases, putting her life and family in danger. This movie is made in the year 1995 .

The Matrix

The iconic sci-fi film explores a simulated reality where humans are unknowningly trapped, and a computer programmer must join a group of rebels to free humanity. This movie is for the year 1999 .

Blackhat(2015)

This movie showcase the cooperation between thye chinese and U.S governments to stop a network of high level cybercrime, highlighthning the global threat of hacking.

Snowden(2016)

Based on real events, the film tells the story of Edward Snowden, who exposed classified document revealing governments surveliance, sparking a national conversation on cybersecurity.
